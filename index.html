About

I'm an artist and creative coder currently studying as an honors student at the University of Montana, where I made Dean's List last semester. Originally from Palm Harbor, Florida, I graduated Summa Cum Laude from East Lake High School.

My work explores the intersection of traditional art-making and digital creation. Whether I'm working with ceramics, illustration, or code, I'm drawn to themes of memory, identity, and the things we carry inside us.
